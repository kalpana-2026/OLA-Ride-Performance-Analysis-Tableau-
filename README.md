# OLA Ride Performance Analysis – Tableau Story
## Project Overview

This project is a comprehensive Tableau Story analyzing OLA ride booking data. It provides end-to-end insights into business performance, ride trends, cancellations, revenue distribution, customer behavior, and operational efficiency.

The story format enables structured storytelling across multiple dashboards for better business understanding.

## Story Structure
## Business Overview

- Total Rides: 1,000+
- Total Revenue: ₹4,01,279+
- Monthly Revenue Trend
- Rides Trend by Month
- Top 5 Pickup Locations
- Total Rides by City

## Operations Analysis
- Ride Status (Completed vs Cancelled)
- Cancellation by Reason
- Revenue by Vehicle Type
- Gender-based Revenue Analysis

## Customer & Driver Insights

- Customer Age Group Analysis
- Driver Rating vs Revenue
- Customer Rating vs Revenue
- Payment Mode Analysis

## Key Insights

- Majority rides were successfully completed.
- Driver delay and high fare were major cancellation reasons.
- Mini and SUV categories generated high revenue.
- High-rated drivers contributed more revenue.
- 26–35 age group showed strong booking activity.
- UPI and Card were commonly used payment modes.
